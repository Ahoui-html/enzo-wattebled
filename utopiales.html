« Les Utopiales » is a French Science Fiction festival in Nantes. It's not a regular festival with cosplay, lights, etc. The festival is known to abord the science fiction through complex thematics and invites philosophers and thinkers to discuss about the theme of the year. Those conferences are quite deep, and the objective of the rebuild of the website is to help the user understand the year's thematic and also, bring some more cool effect, because, Sci-Fi.
