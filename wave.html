W/A/V/E was created on the occasion of Art Macau. It will be exhibited at the House of Dancing Water in City of Dreams, from July 16 to the end of October 2021.

At the basis of this artwork is Thomas Rousselâ€™s musical composition: waves of sound played by a 50-musician orchestra made up exclusively of string instruments (violins, cellos, violas, and double basses).

My role in this project was to explore different 3D landscapes and abstract renders linked to music's emotions and atmosphere.

Videos had been designed by Aranud LAFFOND and me. Through the mediation of computer software made by Martial Geoffre Rouland, the installation tends to never run the same visual twice.
