W/A/V/E was created for the Art Macau event, exhibited at the House of Dancing Water in City of Dreams, from July 16 to the end of October 2021.

The basis of this project is a musical composition by Thomas played by a 50-musician orchestra made up exclusively of string instruments (violins, cellos, violas, and double basses).

My role in this project was to explore and render different landscapes and abstract compositions in 3D that illustrate the different moods of the musical composition.

Videos had been designed by Aranud LAFFOND and me. Martial created software that allows different videos to be merged together. Thanks to this, the installation tends to never run the same visual twice.
