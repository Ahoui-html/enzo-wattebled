After extensive research and analysis of the OFF-WHITE brand, we had to imagine and stage a fashion show for the brand on a miniature model.

We opted for a brutalist setting in an inverted airport. The video mapping simulates the movement of the set (escalator), the typography projections in space, and the passage of the models.
