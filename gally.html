Gally is a vocal assistant made in collaboration with the French insurer MAIF.

This vocal assistant aims to ease access to all the MAIF’s juridic, social content, and videos destined for teachers.

Indeed they often face delicate situations: relations between students or/and parents, harassment… Gally plays the role of confident bound for young teachers who finds themself in an unknown situation. After the conversation, Gally offers documents, pieces of information, or videos linked to the teacher’s situation to guide him in his process.
