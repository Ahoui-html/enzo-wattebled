Objectives

Optimize STONKS company workflow and project management by creating a common platform for traders, Project Managers and freelancers.


Challenges

Respond quickly to customer requests and optimize project tracking

Create different interfaces according to status and rights: salesperson, freelancer, project manager and administrator.

Quickly assign a team and freelancers to a project

Have the project follow-up and exchange with the client centralized on the platform.

Simplify financial management on each project.


Design method and tools

Interviews with the different interns of the company to define their needs on the platform.

Decomposition of the architecture and creation of links between the different parties.

Prioritization of tasks and key functionalities of the application.

Formatting of the functionalities with wireframes.

User tests on the interactive model created on Figma; change contribution and iterations with the team.

Realization of the graphic aspect (precise UI).
