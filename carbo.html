Company background

Carbo supports companies in calculating, analyzing, and reducing their carbon footprint by offering a SaaS application that makes it easy to calculate carbon footprints


Mission

As the company's sole product designer, I have devised various upgrades to our software to provide a smooth and accessible experience for all our users.


Achievements

Creation, documentation, and progressive updating of a Design System on React. Implementation of Flex Responsive, use of variables in Figma.

Redesign of the entire user journey by applying the Design System. Notable areas: Sign-up, Sign-in, Dashboard, Onboarding, Carbon reduction feature, etc.

Planning the migration of the application from Ruby on Rails to React and writing Github tickets focused on front-end developments.

Design thinking: User research, user testing, A/B testing, Figma prototyping, organizing collaborative workshops, and dividing up features to develop the product iteratively.

Working with FrontEnd and BackEnd developers, as well as Product Managers on the various aspects of the application.

Creating a common graphic charter for the company, standardizing our communication materials, and creating simplified product visuals to illustrate our features and strengths.

Collaborating with marketing teams on our landing pages.
